TRANSTU services

The Tunisian Public Transportation (Transtu) manages public mass transit in the greater Tunis area.

Transtu was founded in 2003 when the Société nationale des transports (SNT) and the Société du métro léger de Tunis (SMLT) were combined.

Annually Transtu handles about 460 million passagers.

It supervises the bus network, the light railway system and the TGM light rail link to La Marsa.

Tramway:
The tram of Tunis consists of 6 lines that total 82 km.
Buses:
The bus system covers 5,836 kilometers in Tunis and surroundings serving a population of about 2 million people. 1,050 buses are covering 206 lines.
Train:
TGM is a 19 km 1,435 mm light rail line in Tunisia linking the capital Tunis with La Marsa via La Goulette
